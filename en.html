Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

PillChime is a utility app that helps users manage daily medication reminders. We take your privacy seriously. Please read this privacy policy carefully before using the app.

Our commitment: PillChime does not collect your name, contact information, medication content, or any information that identifies you. We collect only a minimal amount of anonymous usage events (such as "first medication created") via TelemetryDeck, an EU GDPR-compliant service, to improve the product. These events are never linked to your identity.

1. Information We Collect

PillChime does not collect any information that identifies you. Specifically:

  • No account registration required
  • No collection of names, phone numbers, email addresses, or other personal identifiers
  • No location tracking
  • No hardware identifiers (IDFA / IDFV) collected
  • No collection of your medication names, dosages, schedules, or any health data
  • No advertising SDKs

The only information collected is anonymous event telemetry used to measure product activation and improve user experience. See "6. Anonymous Usage Statistics" below.

2. Data Storage

All data you enter in PillChime — including medication details, reminder times, and dose records — is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework.

  • Data is never uploaded to any server
  • Data is never shared with any third party
  • Data is not synced across devices
  • All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

3. Camera Permission

PillChime may request access to your camera solely for taking photos of medications for identification purposes. Photos are saved locally only and are never uploaded to any server. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

4. Notification Permission

PillChime uses iOS system alarms (AlarmKit) and local notifications to remind you to take your medication on time. These reminders are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any network communication.

5. Third-Party Services

PillChime integrates only one privacy-focused third-party service:

TelemetryDeck (anonymous product analytics)

  • Purpose: helps us understand which features are used so we can improve the experience
  • What is collected: see the next section
  • Provider: TelemetryDeck GmbH, headquartered in Augsburg, Germany
  • Servers located in the EU (Germany / Netherlands), protected by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Never linked to your identity, health data, or medication information

PillChime does not use:

  • Advertising services
  • Social login
  • Cloud storage services (note: a future version may integrate iCloud sync; this policy will be updated accordingly)

6. Anonymous Usage Statistics (TelemetryDeck)

To understand whether PillChime meets user needs, we collect a small amount of anonymous usage events via TelemetryDeck.

What we collect:

  • A random UUID generated once per install (contains no personal information; a new UUID is generated if you reinstall the app)
  • Event names (e.g., "first medication created", "first check-in")
  • App version
  • System language
  • Event timestamp

What we do not collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number, or Apple ID
  • Your medication names, dosages, or schedules
  • Any health or medical information
  • Your location
  • Hardware identifiers (IDFA / IDFV)

Data is stored on TelemetryDeck servers in Germany / Netherlands under EU GDPR protection. When you uninstall PillChime, all local data and the anonymous UUID are deleted, and historical event data on TelemetryDeck can no longer be traced back to you.

7. Children's Privacy

PillChime is not designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will post the updated version on this page and update the "Last updated" date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
