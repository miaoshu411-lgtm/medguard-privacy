Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

PillChime is a utility app that helps users manage daily medication reminders. We take your privacy seriously. Please read this privacy policy carefully before using the app.

Our commitment: PillChime (as the developer) does not collect your name, contact information, medication content, or any information that identifies you. We collect only a minimal amount of anonymous usage events (such as "first medication created") via TelemetryDeck, an EU GDPR-compliant service, to improve the product. These events are never linked to your identity. Your medication and reminder data are stored locally by default; if you choose to enable iCloud sync (optional), the data is stored in your own Apple iCloud private space, which the developer still cannot access — see Section 6 for details.

1. Information we collect

PillChime does not collect any information that identifies you. Specifically:

  • No account registration required
  • No collection of names, phone numbers, email addresses, or other personal identifiers
  • No location tracking
  • No hardware identifiers (IDFA / IDFV) collected
  • The developer's servers do not collect your medication names, dosages, schedules, or any health data (if you enable iCloud sync, data is transmitted to your own Apple iCloud account, which the developer likewise cannot access — see Section 6)
  • No advertising SDKs

The only information collected is anonymous event telemetry used to measure product activation and improve user experience. See Section 7, "Anonymous usage statistics", below.

2. Data storage

All data you enter in PillChime — including medication details, reminder times, and dose records — is stored locally on your iPhone by default, using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Local mode (default):

  • Data is never uploaded to any developer-operated server
  • Data is never shared with any third party
  • Local data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

iCloud sync (optional, you can enable or disable it at any time):

  • Data is synced to your own iCloud Private Database via Apple CloudKit
  • Data remains consistent only across Apple devices (iPhone / iPad) signed in with the same Apple ID
  • The developer (PillChime) cannot access, read, or export any data in your iCloud
  • Data is protected by standard Apple iCloud encryption; if you enable Apple's Advanced Data Protection, data becomes end-to-end encrypted
  • See Section 6, "iCloud sync (optional)", below for details

3. Camera permission

PillChime may request access to your camera solely for taking photos of medications for identification purposes. Photos are saved locally (if you enable iCloud sync, photos are synced only to your own Apple iCloud account) and are never uploaded to any developer server. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

4. Notification permission

PillChime uses iOS system alarms (AlarmKit) and local notifications to remind you to take your medication on time. These reminders are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any network communication.

5. Third-party services

PillChime integrates the following third-party services:

1. TelemetryDeck (anonymous product analytics, always on)

  • Purpose: helps us understand which features are used so we can improve the experience
  • What is collected: see Section 7, "Anonymous usage statistics"
  • Provider: TelemetryDeck GmbH, headquartered in Augsburg, Germany
  • Servers located in the EU (Germany / Netherlands), protected by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Never linked to your identity, health data, or medication information

2. Apple iCloud / CloudKit (cloud sync, optional)

  • Purpose: syncs your medication and reminder data across Apple devices signed in with the same Apple ID
  • Provider: Apple Inc.
  • Data is stored in your own iCloud Private Database, protected by standard Apple iCloud encryption
  • The developer (PillChime) cannot read any data in your iCloud
  • Governed by Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • See Section 6, "iCloud sync (optional)", for details

PillChime does not use:

  • Advertising services
  • Social login
  • Third-party cloud storage (e.g., AWS, Alibaba Cloud, Tencent Cloud)

6. iCloud sync (optional)

To help you keep your medication and reminder data consistent across multiple Apple devices, PillChime offers an optional iCloud sync feature.

Enabling and disabling:

  • Enable: iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → PillChime → turn on "Sync this iPhone"
  • Disable: the same path allows you to turn it off at any time; data already on your device is retained and not lost

What is synced:

  • Your medication details (name, dose, notes, medication photos)
  • Alarm and reminder time configuration
  • Dose / check-in records

What we want you to clearly understand:

  • Sync uses Apple's CloudKit Private Database; data is stored in your own iCloud space under your Apple ID
  • Data is only synced between Apple devices (iPhone / iPad) signed in with the same Apple ID and is not shared externally
  • The developer (PillChime) cannot access, read, modify, or export any data in your iCloud — CloudKit Private Database is not visible to app developers by Apple's platform design
  • Data is protected by standard Apple iCloud encryption; if you enable Advanced Data Protection in iOS Settings, the data is end-to-end encrypted, and not even Apple itself can read it
  • iCloud sync is managed by Apple in accordance with its privacy policy

About uninstalling:

  • Uninstalling PillChime removes the local copy on that device
  • Copies already synced to iCloud are not automatically deleted — you can restore them by reinstalling PillChime or signing in on a new device with the same Apple ID, or manually remove them via iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → PillChime

7. Anonymous usage statistics (TelemetryDeck)

To understand whether PillChime meets user needs, we collect a small amount of anonymous usage events via TelemetryDeck.

What we collect:

  • A random UUID generated once per install (contains no personal information; a new UUID is generated if you reinstall the app)
  • Event names (e.g., "first medication created", "first check-in")
  • App version
  • System language
  • Event timestamp

What we do not collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number, or Apple ID
  • Your medication names, dosages, or schedules
  • Any health or medical information
  • Your location
  • Hardware identifiers (IDFA / IDFV)

Data is stored on TelemetryDeck servers in Germany / Netherlands under EU GDPR protection. When you uninstall PillChime, all local data and the anonymous UUID are deleted, and historical event data on TelemetryDeck can no longer be traced back to you.

8. Children's privacy

PillChime is not designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
