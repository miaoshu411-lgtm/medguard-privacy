Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

PillChime is a utility app that helps users manage daily medication reminders. We take your privacy seriously. Please read this privacy policy carefully before using the app.

Our commitment: PillChime does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal data. All your information is stored exclusively on your own device.

1. Information We Collect

PillChime does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration required
  • No collection of names, phone numbers, email addresses, or other personal identifiers
  • No location tracking
  • No device identifier collection
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

2. Data Storage

All data you enter in PillChime — including medication details, reminder times, and dose records — is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework.

  • Data is never uploaded to any server
  • Data is never shared with any third party
  • Data is not synced across devices
  • All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

3. Camera Permission

PillChime may request access to your camera solely for taking photos of medications for identification purposes. Photos are saved locally only and are never uploaded to any server. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

4. Notification Permission

PillChime uses iOS system alarms (AlarmKit) and local notifications to remind you to take your medication on time. These reminders are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any network communication.

5. Third-Party Services

PillChime does not integrate any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • No third-party analytics tools
  • No advertising services
  • No social login
  • No cloud storage services

6. Children's Privacy

PillChime is not designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will post the updated version on this page and update the "Last updated" date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
